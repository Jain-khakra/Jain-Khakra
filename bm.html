Bajri Methi Khakra – A Wholesome & Flavorful Millet Delight/h1> Delight in the timeless flavors of tradition with our Bajri Methi Khakra, a beautifully handcrafted snack that brings together the earthy richness of bajra (pearl millet) and the aromatic warmth of fresh methi (fenugreek) leaves. Each khakra is lovingly rolled and slow-roasted to golden perfection, offering an exquisite crunch that awakens your senses with every bite.

Bajra, known as an ancient superfood, is naturally packed with fiber, protein, and essential minerals, making this snack a wholesome and nourishing indulgence. The deep, nutty essence of bajra flour is elevated by the slightly bitter-sweet notes of methi, infused with a delicate medley of traditional Indian spices that enhance the flavor profile without overpowering its natural goodness.

Whether paired with a steaming cup of chai, creamy yogurt, or tangy pickles, this khakra is a versatile delight that fits into any moment of your day—be it a morning energy boost, a midday treat, or a light evening munch. Lightweight, travel-friendly, and crafted without artificial preservatives, our Bajri Methi Khakra is the perfect balance of authentic taste, crunch, and nourishment.

A fusion of heritage and health, every bite of Bajri Methi Khakra tells a story—of tradition, taste, and pure delight!
