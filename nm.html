Nachni Masala Khakra – A Spicy & Wholesome Superfood Snack

Bring home the goodness of nachni (ragi) with a spicy twist in our Nachni Masala Khakra, a perfectly crispy and nutritious snack packed with fiber, protein, and essential minerals. Made from premium ragi flour and infused with a flavorful mix of Indian spices, this khakra is hand-rolled and slow-roasted to perfection, offering a deliciously crunchy bite every time.

Ragi is a superfood known for its high calcium, iron, and antioxidant content, making this khakra an ideal guilt-free alternative to unhealthy snacking. The earthy richness of nachni is complemented by a zesty blend of cumin, chili powder, black pepper, and turmeric, adding an irresistible spicy kick to every bite.

Pair it with chai, yogurt, pickles, or enjoy it as a standalone snack—its bold flavors and crisp texture make it perfect for anytime munching. Lightweight, travel-friendly, and free from artificial preservatives, Nachni Masala Khakra is a tasty yet healthy snack that keeps you full and energized throughout the day.

Nutritious, crunchy, and full of flavor—Nachni Masala Khakra is the ultimate snack for spice lovers and health-conscious foodies!
