Cholafali Khakra – A Crispy & Spicy Chickpea Delight

Indulge in the authentic taste of Gujarat with our Cholafali Khakra, a light and crispy snack made from premium chana (chickpea) flour, garnished with a tantalizing blend of red chili powder and rock salt. Carefully handcrafted and slow-roasted to perfection, this khakra offers an ultra-thin, airy crunch with a burst of bold and balanced flavors in every bite.

Chana flour provides a protein-rich, fiber-packed base, making this khakra a wholesome alternative to fried snacks. The spicy heat of red chili powder, complemented by the mineral-rich goodness of rock salt, enhances the flavor while keeping it light and easy to digest. Whether paired with tea, buttermilk, chutneys, or pickles, or simply enjoyed on its own, Cholafali Khakra is a delicious, guilt-free treat for any time of the day.

Lightweight, travel-friendly, and free from artificial preservatives, this khakra is perfect for those who love bold flavors with a touch of tradition. Enjoy the perfect crunch, spice, and goodness of rock salt with every bite of our Cholafali Khakra—crispy, spicy, and absolutely irresistible!
