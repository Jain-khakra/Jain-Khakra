Methi Masala Khakra – A Perfect Blend of Crunch, Spice & Goodness

Savor the authentic taste of India with our Methi Masala Khakra, a deliciously crunchy snack made from premium wheat flour, infused with fresh methi (fenugreek) leaves and a rich blend of traditional Indian spices. Each khakra is hand-rolled and slow-roasted to achieve a perfect golden crisp, offering a satisfying crunch with every bite.

The earthy aroma of methi, combined with the bold flavors of cumin, turmeric, chili, and black pepper, creates a mouthwatering taste that lingers. Methi is known for its numerous health benefits, including aiding digestion and supporting overall well-being, making this khakra not just tasty but also nutritious. With the wholesomeness of wheat flour and the warmth of aromatic spices, this snack is a perfect guilt-free alternative to fried snacks.

Enjoy it with chai, pickles, chutneys, or yogurt, or relish it straight from the pack—it’s a versatile treat that fits any occasion. Whether you're at home, at work, or traveling, this lightweight and travel-friendly snack is a convenient and flavorful choice for anytime munching.

No artificial preservatives, just pure taste and crunch! Our Methi Masala Khakra is packed fresh to retain its natural flavors and crispiness. Experience the perfect balance of spice, health, and tradition with every bite!
