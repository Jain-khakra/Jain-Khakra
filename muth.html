Muth Khakra – A Timeless Blend of Tradition, Flavor & Wellness

Indulge in the earthy richness and wholesome nourishment of our Muth Khakra, a crispy, handcrafted masterpiece made from the superfood horse gram (muth) flour. Revered in ancient Indian traditions for its incredible nutritional benefits, muth is a powerhouse of protein, fiber, and iron, known to energize the body, aid digestion, and promote overall well-being. Every bite of this khakra offers a delightful crunch, carrying the essence of time-honored recipes blended with the magic of modern craftsmanship.

The deep, nutty undertones of muth flour are perfectly complemented by a delicate symphony of traditional Indian spices, each ingredient carefully selected to elevate its natural goodness. The khakra is slow-roasted to golden perfection, ensuring that its light, crispy texture and bold flavors come together in an exquisite harmony that dances on the palate. The gentle heat from the spices, paired with the heartiness of horse gram, makes for a satisfying and deeply comforting snacking experience.

Versatile and incredibly delicious, Muth Khakra is a perfect companion for every moment—whether you’re starting your morning with a cup of masala chai, enjoying a midday break with refreshing buttermilk, or winding down with a side of tangy pickles and yogurt. It is an ideal snack for those who seek both taste and nourishment, offering wholesome satisfaction without any guilt.

Lightweight, travel-friendly, and completely free from artificial preservatives, this khakra is designed to fit seamlessly into your busy lifestyle while keeping you energized, fulfilled, and craving just one more bite. Let the timeless flavors of tradition meet the crisp perfection of modern snacking—because every bite of Muth Khakra is a celebration of heritage, health, and pure indulgence.

Savor the crunch, embrace the nourishment, and experience the soulful goodness of Muth Khakra—where taste meets tradition in every crisp, golden bite!
