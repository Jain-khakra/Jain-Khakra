Spiced Masala Khakra – A Flavorful Crunch with Rich Indian Spices

Experience the perfect fusion of taste and crunch with our Spiced Masala Khakra, crafted from premium wheat flour and infused with a blend of rich, aromatic Indian spices. Each khakra is carefully hand-rolled and slow-roasted to perfection, delivering a golden-brown crispness that is both light and satisfying. Packed with bold flavors, this khakra offers a delightful balance of warmth, zest, and a hint of spice in every bite.

Made with a thoughtfully selected mix of traditional spices, including cumin, turmeric, black pepper, and chili, this khakra not only enhances your taste buds but also provides numerous health benefits. The wholesome wheat flour adds fiber and essential nutrients, making it a nutritious and guilt-free alternative to fried snacks. Whether paired with chai, pickles, chutneys, or yogurt, or enjoyed on its own, this khakra is a versatile, all-time favorite snack for spice lovers.

Free from artificial preservatives and packed fresh to retain its crunch and authentic flavors, our Spiced Masala Khakra is the ideal choice for those who appreciate the rich, bold taste of traditional Indian seasonings. Lightweight and travel-friendly, it makes for a perfect companion on busy workdays, road trips, or casual snacking at home.

Indulge in a flavorful, crispy, and wholesome experience with every bite—Spiced Masala Khakra: where health meets taste!
